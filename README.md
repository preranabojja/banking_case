

# Banking Customer Analytics & Risk Intelligence | EDA, SQL, Tableau, Machine Learning & FinTech Analytics
## Executive Summary 
This project is an end-to-end banking and fintech analytics platform built using Python, PostgreSQL, SQL, machine learning, and Tableau. The goal of the project was to analyze customer profitability, financial risk exposure, product penetration, and customer behavior through exploratory data analysis (EDA), predictive modeling, and executive dashboarding. A PostgreSQL database and SQL analytics pipeline were developed to perform customer segmentation, KPI reporting, and risk analysis, while machine learning models were used for customer churn and customer value prediction. The final Tableau dashboard provides an interactive executive view of customer profitability, risk exposure, income trends, and banking performance metrics. Key findings revealed that high-income customers generated greater customer value, high-risk customers carried larger loan balances, and several customer segments presented strong cross-selling opportunities. Future improvements include real-time data pipelines, automated reporting workflows, and advanced customer lifetime value forecasting.
## Business Problem
Financial institutions generate massive amounts of customer data but often struggle to transform it into actionable business insights.
Key challenges:
- Understanding customer profitability
- Monitoring financial risk exposure
- Identifying high-value banking customers
- Improving executive reporting visibility

## Results & Recommendations
Key Results
Jade-tier customers generated the highest customer value
Higher-income customers showed stronger profitability
High-risk customers carried larger loan balances
Product adoption opportunities remained underutilized
Recommendations
Prioritize retention of high-value customers
Strengthen monitoring of high-risk loan groups
Expand targeted cross-selling campaigns
Enhance executive KPI reporting
